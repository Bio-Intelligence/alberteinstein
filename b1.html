Early Life

Albert Einstein was born on March 14, 1879, in Ulm, Germany, to a middle-class Jewish family. He showed an early interest in science and mathematics, particularly fascinated by a geometry book at the age of twelve. Despite his brilliance, he struggled in school due to a rigid educational system that did not cater to his curiosity.Albert Einstein was born to Hermann Einstein and Pauline Koch. His father managed a featherbed business, which later failed, leading the family to move to Munich. Einstein had one sister, Maria (nicknamed Maja), who was two years younger than him. The family was secular Jewish, and both parents were educated; his mother was known for her musical talent, while his father had a keen interest in mathematics.
